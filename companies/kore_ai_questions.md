# Suggested Interview Questions - Kore.ai

## Experience & Background
- Walk me through your background
- What are your biggest accomplishments?
- What makes you a great CMO?
- Tell me about your experience with pipeline generation for enterprise sales

## Marketing Philosophy & Approach
- What is your philosophy on what marketing does?
- What's your 30/60/90 day plan?
- How do you position technical AI platforms to non-technical buyers?
- Tell me about your approach to analyst relations with Gartner and Forrester

## GTM Strategy for Kore.ai
- How would you approach the first 90 days as CMO at Kore.ai?
- How do you fix a brand awareness problem?
- How do you balance technical depth with market accessibility?
- How would you position Kore.ai against competitors in conversational AI?

## Team & Organization
- How do you prioritize marketing hiring for enterprise platforms?
- What qualities do you look for in marketing leaders?
- What is your approach to building teams for technical products?
- How would you structure marketing for enterprise direct sales?

## Category Creation & Market Leadership
- How do you create and own a new market category?
- What is your approach to working with Gartner?
- How do you turn analyst recognition into pipeline?
- Tell me about building thought leadership for technical platforms
